About Us

Monastery360, is dedicated to digitally preserving and showcasing Sikkim’s monasteries. Using AR/VR, 360° imaging, and cloud technology, we create immersive virtual tours of sacred murals, manuscripts, and artifacts. Our mission is to make this heritage accessible worldwide, fostering cultural awareness, tourism, education, and research, while carrying the timeless spirit of Sikkim into the digital age.

Directorate of Handicrafts & Handloom

A visit to the Directorate of Handicrafts & Handloom is a journey into Sikkim's heart. Every handwoven carpet, intricate painting, and carved artifact tells a story of tradition. It's the perfect place to find a truly authentic souvenir and support local artisans.

Shopping paradise awaits!

Goechala Trek

Dare to stand face to face with giants on the Goechala Trek, a journey that takes you to the very soul of Sikkim. This is your chance to witness the majestic Mount Kanchenjunga up close, in a way you've never seen it before, on a trek that promises unforgettable, panoramic views.
Yuksom-Dzongri Trek

For those seeking the perfect Himalayan introduction, the Yuksom-Dzongri Trek delivers all the views in half the time. Every step on this trail reveals a new masterpiece, making it your ultimate gateway to the breathtaking peaks of the Eastern Himalayas.
Barsey Rhododendron Trek

Imagine a walk through a living painting. The Barsey Rhododendron Trek immerses you in a sea of blooms, offering a nature's spectacle that is both vibrant and unfiltered. It's a journey through Sikkim's floral paradise, where the beauty of the landscape takes your breath away.
Momo

More than a snack, momos are the soul of Sikkim. These steamed dumplings, filled with meat or vegetables and served with a zesty chutney, are the ultimate comfort food. One bite, and you'll understand why they're a local favorite.
Thukpa

A warm hug in a bowl! Thukpa is a hearty noodle soup that's perfect for a chilly day in the mountains. This soulful dish, with its flavorful broth and rich ingredients, is a true taste of authentic Himalayan tradition.

Monastery360 is a digital heritage platform by Team ABHYAS that preserves and showcases over 200 monasteries of Sikkim through immersive 360° virtual tours, digitized art, manuscripts, and cultural artifacts.

It helps preserve Sikkim’s spiritual and cultural heritage,promotes tourism, and provides valuable resources for researchers, students, and cultural enthusiasts worldwide.

We use AR/VR, 360° imaging, cloud technology, and multilingual integration to create authentic, accessible, and engaging digital monastery experiences.

Tourists, researchers, educators, students, and local communities — anyone interested in exploring and learning about Sikkim’s monasteries and cultural legacy.
